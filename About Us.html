About Us

Welcome to the ATAR Design Simulated Workplace

ATAR Design is a Registered Training Organisation. Our programs are nationally recognised training packages providing high quality training that meet the Australian Qualification Framework (AQF) standards.

ATAR Design is dedicated to providing quality training and assessment for students who wish to gain a formal qualification. Our training is delivered by passionate people who actually worked in industry.

How to use this site

This website has been designed for ATAR Design students to practice skills and develop knowledge about RTO compliance and Management. This site is a ficticious RTO containing all the resources you'll need to complete your simulated learning activites.

The site is made up of 3 key areas

  • Busines website:

    The site is set-up as a regular website that clients and prospective students might visit to find information about courses on offer.

  • Student Portal:

    The student portal takes you to information required and accessed by RTO enrolled students. You'll be able to access student handbooks, policies and procedures and course materials in this section.

  • Staff Portal:

    This area of the site gives you access to internal RTO resources such as forms and templates, policies and procedures and the documents you might normally find in an RTO computer server or intranet.

We hope you enjoy exploring our simulated RTO workplace.
